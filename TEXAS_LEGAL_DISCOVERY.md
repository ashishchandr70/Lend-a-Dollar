# Texas Legal Discovery Checklist (Platform as Lender)

## 1. Lending License
- Does Texas require a consumer lending license for this model?
- Which agency issues it?
- Application timeline and cost?
- Net worth or bonding requirements?

## 2. Interest & Usury Caps
- Texas interest rate limits for small-dollar loans
- Is inflation-linked interest permissible?
- Are there special rules for installment loans?

## 3. Consumer Disclosures
- Required disclosures at loan origination
- Plain-language requirements
- Cooling-off periods (if any)

## 4. Collections & Defaults
- What collections practices are legally permitted?
- Is “no collections” legally acceptable?
- Reporting obligations for write-offs?

## 5. Money Transmission Risk
- Would holding pooled funds trigger MSB / money transmitter rules?
- Custody implications
- Stablecoin custody considerations (future)

## 6. AML / KYC
- If platform is lender of record, what AML/KYC is required?
- Minimum identity verification standards
- Recordkeeping obligations

## 7. Data Privacy
- Texas data privacy laws applicable?
- Federal privacy obligations (GLBA)
- Data retention rules

## 8. AI / Fair Lending
- Fair lending laws (ECOA)
- Bias and explainability requirements
- Right to explanation & appeals compliance

## 9. Consumer Protection
- CFPB jurisdiction applicability
- Complaint handling requirements
- Audit readiness

## 10. Pilot Constraints (If Ever Launched)
- Max loan size
- Borrower caps
- Manual review requirement
- Kill switch
- Public transparency report
